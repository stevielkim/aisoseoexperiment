For optimal heart health, focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats, while limiting saturated and trans fats, sodium, and added sugars.

Specific diets like the DASH diet and Mediterranean diet are highly recommended for their heart-healthy benefits.
Key Principles of a Heart-Healthy Diet:
  • Prioritize Plant Foods: Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, beans, and nuts.
  • Choose Lean Protein: Opt for fish, poultry (skinless), and lean meats.
  • Healthy Fats: Incorporate omega-3 fatty acids from oily fish, olive oil, nuts, and seeds.
  • Limit Saturated and Trans Fats: Reduce intake of saturated fats from red meat, full-fat dairy, and processed foods.
  • Control Sodium: Limit processed foods, fast food, and salty snacks.
  • Reduce Added Sugars: Minimize sugary drinks, sweets, and refined carbohydrates.
Specific Diets for Heart Health:
DASH Diet:
Emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and low-fat dairy while limiting sodium, saturated fat, and red meat.
Mediterranean Diet:
Similar to the DASH diet, with a focus on plant-based foods, olive oil, and fish, while limiting processed foods, red meat, and saturated fat.
Foods to Include:
  • Fruits and Vegetables: A wide variety of colors and types, including leafy greens, broccoli, carrots, apples, bananas, oranges, etc.
  • Whole Grains: Oatmeal, brown rice, whole-grain bread, and pasta.
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, olive oil, nuts, seeds, and oily fish.
  • Lean Proteins: Salmon, tuna, trout, skinless chicken, turkey, eggs, and lentils.
Foods to Limit or Avoid:
  • Saturated Fats: Red meat, butter, full-fat dairy, and coconut oil.
  • Trans Fats: Processed foods, fried foods, and baked goods.
  • Sodium: Processed foods, fast food, and salty snacks.
  • Added Sugars: Sugary drinks, sweets, and refined carbohydrates.
Additional Tips:
  • Read Labels: Pay attention to sodium, saturated fat, and added sugar content on food labels.
  • Prepare Meals at Home: This allows you to control ingredients and portion sizes.
  • Snack Smart: Choose healthy snacks like fruits, vegetables, nuts, and yogurt.
  • Be Mindful of Alcohol: If you drink alcohol, do so in moderation.
