Elaboration:
Fatigue and Muscle Weakness:
A common symptom is persistent fatigue or feeling unusually tired, along with muscle weakness, especially in the legs.
Bone Pain and Softening:
Vitamin D is crucial for bone health, and deficiency can lead to bone pain, softening (osteomalacia), and increased risk of fractures.
Increased Illness:
Vitamin D plays a role in the immune system, so deficiency can make individuals more susceptible to colds and other infections.
Mood Changes:
Some research suggests a link between vitamin D deficiency and mood disorders like depression or irritability.
Hair Loss:
Hair loss can be another symptom of vitamin D deficiency.
Sleep Problems:
Difficulty sleeping or experiencing poor sleep quality may also be associated with vitamin D deficiency.
Other Potential Symptoms:
Other potential symptoms include weight gain, impaired wound healing, and back pain.
