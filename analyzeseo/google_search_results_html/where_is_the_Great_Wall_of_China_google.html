The Great Wall of China is located in northern ChinaThe Great Wall of China is located in northern China, stretching across 15 provinces and autonomous regions.
Show more
Show less
It runs from the Bohai Sea in the east to the Gobi Desert in the west, a distance of about 2,500 kilometers.
The best-known sections are around Beijing, including Badaling and Mutianyu.
More details:
  • Eastern End: Shanhaiguan in Hebei province, near the Bohai Sea.
  • Western End: Jiayuguan in Gansu province, near the Gobi Desert.
  • Provinces and Regions: The wall traverses Hebei, Shanxi, Inner Mongolia, Liaoning, Gansu, Ningxia, Shaanxi, and other regions.
  • Key Sections: Badaling (most accessible), Mutianyu (popular and restored), Simatai (rugged), Jinshanling (for hikers).
  • Length: The total length is estimated to be over 21,000 kilometers (13,170.70 mi), according to Wikipedia.
